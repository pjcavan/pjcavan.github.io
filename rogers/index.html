Fourth Time Lucky
November 20, 2010

Rogers

Between:

  • my Rogers-supplied phone silently dropping texts because, despite being introduced in 2010, it can only hold a total of 200
  • my Rogers plan silently dropping texts and calls because, without any notification, the plan expired a day early

I really wonder how I get any communication done in Canada.
