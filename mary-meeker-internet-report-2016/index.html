Mary Meeker Internet Report, 2016

Mary Meeker factoids for 2016:


Internet: 42% (3bn) of world's population has access to the internet.

Advertising: 76% (and rising) of US internet advertising growth is going to just two companies - Google and Facebook. Mobile adblocking is up 94% to 420m people.

Commerce: Almost 11% of US retail sales in 2015 were online, up from 1.5% in 2000. Warby Parker achieves more sales per sqft of retail space ($3000) than Tiffany's ($2951).

AI: Google voice search has taken off since 2013, but "Call mom" still twice as popular as "call dad".

China: Added more gross capital formation in last six years ($21trn) than previous thirty ($20trn). 87% of GDP growth coming from services, not manufacturing. 71% of time spent on mobiles is on the properties of only three groups - Tencent, Alibaba, Baidu. 70% of world's on-demand transportation trips in Q1 2016 were taken in China.
