Nausea

  • Tends to peak at 8-9 weeks then decline around 13-14
  • 5% throw up once a day for a month
  • 1% are hospitalized
  • B6 helpful. Ginger doubtful

Safe drugs

  • Claritin
  • Benadryl
  • Tums
  • Pepcid AC
  • Tylenol

Screening

  • Cell-free fetal DNA via blood test (NIPS/NITS) - quite accurate, not always covered by insurance.
  • Ultrasound (NT) + blood test (quad screen) - less accurate, cheaper
  • CVS/amnio - accurate, small increased risk of miscarriage (1 in 800)
